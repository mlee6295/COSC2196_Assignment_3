Group Processes

The members of Team_Name_404 for Assignment 3, worked very well together in Assignment 2. We took time in our first group meeting for Assignment 3 to reflect on what worked for the previous assessment, and what we would change going forward. We decided to continue with the following processes:

  • No set ‘team leader’, instead each member responsible for sharing the workload.
  • Streamline channels on MS Teams, using a General channel for chat and relevant channels only for uploading relevant files.
  • Use MS Teams as main point of contact for all assignment related correspondence.
  • Schedule meetings either once or twice a week to be conducted through MS Teams.
  • Each team member to commit their own work to Github.
  • Each team member to supply references for assignment report bibliography.

Further details on Group Processes for Team_Name_404 are outlined in ‘Group Processes and Communications’ under ‘Project Description’.
