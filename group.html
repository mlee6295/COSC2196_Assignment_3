Key Group Takeaways from Assignment 3

As a group, we think Team_Name_404 worked very well together for Assignment 3 & 5. We were all able to participate regularly in the weekly or twice weekly meetings, taking turns to Chair and record Minutes/Actions. We all feel the work was divided well within the group across the whole assignment brief, and we each were able to learn new skills and develop our strengths in web design, wireframing, researching and writing. Each member was respectful and patient with others, and brought a lot of energy and great ideas for the concept development of Yogi.

The main hurdle we found in working towards completion of Assignment 3 and 5 was time management. We found that as a team we were still working on things right up until the due date, and it did create stress for team members in finishing our Assignment to the high standards we expected of ourselves. It was a lesson for us all in juggling study with our work/life commitments and COVID lockdowns, as well as making time for weekly unit participation and completing assignments in all of our other subjects.

The one thing that was most surprising for Team_Name_404 was again related to time management. How long things like layout, formatting, and report construction took was surprising. Coding was also very time consuming, in making the Assignment 3 website in GitHub, as well as wireframing for the Yogi prototype. This required members to also learn new skills, and perhaps the amount of work needed for Assignment 3 and 5 was underestimated.

Members of Team_Name_404 have learned to be very specific about which work is being done by which team member. We have also learned that more aggressive timelines for group completion of work is required going forward, as well as perhaps the need for appointing a team leader. Overall, Team_Name_404 has learned that working in groups can certainly have its funny moments, and how they can each fit into group dynamics. We have learned that we now have a fantastic project concept, that we have enjoyed developing together and we look forward to continuing as a group past the completion of Assignment 3 & 5 to bring Yogi to life!
