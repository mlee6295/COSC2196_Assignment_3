WEEK 9 - PROGRESS

Concept

While awaiting feedback from A2 on our project idea, we decided that we needed more design ideas for the layout and ‘look’ of our app. This included colours, fonts, possible logo/graphic ideas.

The project was still very much focused primarily on connecting services of dog walkers and pet sitters with dogs and owners in their respective areas. There was an idea that revenue would be created through advertising by relevant industry services in the app. The Tinder-inspired swipe left or right for matching dog walkers with the dog profiles was a desired function for the app.

At this stage, the thought of us building an app was daunting for the group with our current skill level. We realized that we needed to understand and do further research on the technical aspects of building an app (see below in Content) before the next meeting.
