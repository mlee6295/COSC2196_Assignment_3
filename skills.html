Skills and Jobs

Project Manager

We at Team_Name_404 are looking for Project Manager to help us take charge of our start-up project and take it to the next level. We are looking for someone who is enthusiastic, pro-active and has the willingness to collaborate with a small team of up and comers. This will be a great opportunity for someone to work on new projects within an innovative team of developers. This position is suited to an individual who has strong leadership skills and has experience overseeing a small team.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Leading and managing small to large scale projects
  • Creates and implements project definitions, schedules, budgets, and objectives for small to large sized projects. Monitors performance to ensure project meets set specifications. Determines need for and impact of changes in scope of project and makes recommendations based on determinations
  • Oversees a small team assigned to projects and tasks. Addresses performance issues and provides direction with regards to timeliness and completion of project objectives. Provides performance input upon completion of project
  • Perform regular ongoing project review to ensure deadlines are met to scope, budget and schedule

The successful candidate will have the following skills:

  • 5 years’ experience as an IT Project Manager
  • Bachelor’s degree in Project Management, IT or equivalent and/or experience in related field
  • Strong technical IT background with broad, stable industry experience
  • Good communication skills
  • Experience with productivity and methodology tools that increase project efficiency and effectiveness
  • Experience working with delivery assurance principles and appropriate procedures relevant to area
